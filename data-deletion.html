If you want to delete your data from CommentSystem, please follow these steps:

Automatic Data Deletion:

  1. Go to your Facebook Settings
  2. Navigate to "Apps and Websites"
  3. Find "commentsystem" in the list
  4. Click "Remove" to revoke access
  5. Your data will be automatically deleted within 30 days

Manual Data Deletion Request:

Send an email to: domisovandara@gmail.com

Include:

  • Your Facebook User ID
  • Email address used with our app
  • Reason for deletion (optional)

What Data We Delete:

  • Your Facebook profile information
  • Page access tokens
  • Comment and post data
  • Usage analytics

Processing Time: Data deletion requests are processed within 30 days.
